MODULE: REPORTING & LEDGER (TRANSACTIONS) - EXHAUSTIVE DETAILS

1. OVERVIEW
The Reporting & Ledger module (internally named `Transactions`) is the analytical brain of StockDo. While other modules record data, this module interprets it to produce financial statements, dues lists, tax summaries, and executive dashboards.

2. THE "UNIVERSAL LEDGER" CONCEPT
Architecture: StockDo uses a unified `transactions` table to track every financial event.
- Single Source of Truth: Whether it is a Bill (Sales), a Purchase (Acquisition), a Receipt (Cash in), or a Payment (Cash out), it is logged here.
- Entity Agnostic: The `ledgerAccountId` can represent a Customer, Supplier, or Employee. This allows for a "Master Ledger" that can track the company's net position against any party in the system.

3. FILE-BY-FILE CODE EXPLANATION

--- backend/src/transactions/transactions.service.ts ---
* Logic: Dynamic Ledger Replay (`getLedger`)
  - Calculation: The system does not store a static "Closing Balance" (which could become corrupt). Instead, it "replays" every transaction from day zero to calculate the Opening Balance for any requested period.
  - Perspective Switch: It automatically flips the "Debit/Credit" logic based on the party type.
    - For Customers: Bills = Debit (+), Receipts = Credit (-).
    - For Suppliers: Bills (Purchases) = Credit (+), Payments = Debit (-).
  - Enrichment: It cross-references `billId` to show literal Bill Numbers instead of cryptic UUIDs in the ledger rows.

* Logic: The Dues List (`getDueslist`)
  - Scaling: Uses Prisma `groupBy` directly in Postgres to aggregate hundreds of transactions into a single "Net Position" per customer.
  - Advance Handling: It explicitly identifies "Advance Payments" (where receipts exceed bills) to help management prioritize collections.

* Logic: Balance Sheet Engine (`getBalanceSheet`)
  - Asset Valuation: 
    - Fixed Assets: Pulled from Company settings.
    - Inventory Value: Dynamically calculated by multiplying `material.totalQty` by the "Latest Purchase Price" from the Purchases module.
    - Cash on Hand: Calculated by replaying all historical Receipt/Payment transactions.
  - Equity Balance: It uses the accounting equation: `Capital = Assets - Liabilities` to show the owner's net equity in the business.

* Logic: GST Engine (`getGstSummary`)
  - Tax Mapping: Aggregates CGST, SGST, and IGST components from both Sales (Bills) and Purchases.
  - Result: Provides a clean summary of "GST Collected" vs "GST Paid," allowing the firm to quickly calculate their tax liability for the month.

4. CORE BUSINESS LOGIC & FLOW
1. Event Trigger: A Bill is finalized or a Receipt is recorded.
2. Ledger Post: A row is inserted into the `transactions` table.
3. Pulse Check: The Dashboard module calls `getDashboardSummary` to update the earnings/spendings charts.
4. Compliance: At month-end, the user downloads the `gst-summary` and `daybook` to provide to their accounting department or auditor.
5. Strategic View: The `annual-report` provides management with a 12-month bird's-eye view of profitability and material damage/loss trends.
