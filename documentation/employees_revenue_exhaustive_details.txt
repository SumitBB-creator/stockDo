MODULE: EMPLOYEES & DAILY REVENUE - EXHAUSTIVE DETAILS

1. OVERVIEW
This documentation covers the internal operational modules: Employee management (staff records and payroll ledger) and Daily Revenue (automated projected earnings calculations).

2. DATABASE TABLES
--- Table: employees ---
* Purpose: Stores master records for staff members.
* Critical Fields:
  - name / phone / email: Basic identity.
  - ledgerAccountId (String, Unique): Ensures the employee is a valid "entity" in the general ledger for financial transactions like salary advances or travel reimbursements.
  - relationType / relationName: Legal identification (e.g., S/O for Son of).
  - pan / gstIn: For tax compliance.

--- Table: daily_revenue ---
* Purpose: Stores a daily financial snapshot of the company's "Rent in Progress."
* Fields:
  - date: The specific snapshot date.
  - amount: Total rental value of all materials currently at all customer sites.

3. FILE-BY-FILE CODE EXPLANATION

--- backend/src/employees/employees.service.ts ---
* Logic: Shared Ledger ID Sequence
  - To prevent ID collisions between Customers, Suppliers, and Employees, the `create` method scans all three tables to find the current maximum `LAID`.
  - It increments this value and pads it to 6 digits (e.g., `LAID000142`).
  - This ensures that if you pay an employee, it is recorded in the same unified transaction system as a customer payment.

--- backend/src/daily-revenue/daily-revenue.service.ts ---
* Logic: The Prediction Engine (`handleDailyRevenueCalculation`)
  - Frequency: Runs every night at midnight via `@Cron`.
  - Methodology:
    1. It fetches all "Active" agreements.
    2. For each agreement, it scans all historical Challans (Issues and Returns) to determine the exact "Stock on Site" at that moment.
    3. It then multiplies that quantity by the "Hire Rate" defined in the specific agreement.
    4. Result: It generates a `totalDailyRevenue` which represents what the company earned *today* in rent, even if a bill hasn't been generated yet.
* Logic: Dashboard Support (`getRevenueTrend`)
  - Fetches the last 30 snapshots to provide the data for the "Revenue Growth" chart on the main dashboard.

4. CORE BUSINESS LOGIC & FLOW
1. Staff Onboarding: New employees are added, and a unique Ledger ID is reserved for them.
2. Financial Fairness: Any cash given to an employee for fuel or site expenses is recorded against their Ledger ID.
3. Revenue Visibility: While the Billing module shows "Historical Cash," the Daily Revenue module provides "Real-time Accrual." This allows management to see if revenue is increasing or decreasing based on stock movement *before* the month ends.
