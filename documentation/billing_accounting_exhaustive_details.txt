MODULE: BILLING & ACCOUNTING - EXHAUSTIVE DETAILS

1. OVERVIEW
The Billing & Accounting module is the financial engine of StockDo. It transforms physical stock movements (Challans) into monetary invoices (Bills) and tracks those invoices against payments in a double-entry style Ledger system.

2. DATABASE TABLES
--- Table: bills ---
* Purpose: Stores finalized monthly or custom-range invoices.
* Critical Fields:
  - billNumber (String, e.g., "BILL00042"): Sequential identifier.
  - dateFrom / dateTo: The period covered by the invoice.
  - totalAmount: The base rent calculated for the period.
  - taxableAmount: The amount on which GST is applied (includes transport if configured).
  - GST Fields: cgst, sgst, igst, gstType, gstRate.
  - grandTotal: The final "Payable" amount including taxes and logistic costs.
* Relationships:
  - items: Every line item in a bill represents a material's rent for a specific duration within the month.

--- Table: transactions ---
* Purpose: The "General Ledger" recording every increase or decrease in a party's balance.
* Types:
  - BILL: Debits the customer (Increases debt).
  - RECEIPT: Credits the customer (Decreases debt).
  - PAYMENT: Used for refunds or supplier payments.
  - CREDIT_NOTE / DEBIT_NOTE: For manual adjustments.
* Critical Fields:
  - ledgerAccountId: Can be the Customer UUID or the human-readable LAID.
  - amount: The absolute value of the transaction.
  - referenceId: Links back to the original Bill or Receipt.

3. FILE-BY-FILE CODE EXPLANATION

--- backend/src/billing/billing.service.ts ---
* Logic: The Rent Engine (`calculateHandlingPeriods`)
  - This method uses a **FIFO (First-In, First-Out)** stock model.
  - It maintains a `stockState` (Map) of open material issues.
  - When a "Return" challan occurs, it deducts the quantity from the oldest recorded "Issue" for that material.
  - Duration Calculation: It splits the billing month into segments based on challan dates. If a customer has 100 pipes for 10 days and then returns 50, the algorithm creates two segments: (100 qty * 10 days) + (50 qty * 20 days).
  - Full-Month Logic: Ensures that a full month (e.g., 1st to 30th) is counted as 30 days of rent, not 29, using specific `date-fns` difference logic.

* Logic: The GST Engine (`createBill`)
  - Rule 1: It compares the Company's State vs. the Customer's Office State.
  - Rule 2 (Intra-State): If states match, it applies CGST (9%) + SGST (9%).
  - Rule 3 (Inter-State): If states differ, it applies IGST (18%).
  - Rule 4 (Transporters): It checks `company.gstOnTransportation`. If true, transport costs are added to the taxable base; if false, they are added after GST.

--- backend/src/transactions/transactions.service.ts ---
* Logic: Ledger Replay (`getLedger`)
  - To ensure 100% accuracy, the system does not just store a "Balance" field. It calculates the balance on-the-fly.
  - Step 1: Accumulate all transactions prior to the requested Date Range to find the "Opening Balance".
  - Step 2: Iterate through the period, adding/subtracting based on type.
  - Mirroring: For Customers, BILLS are positive (Debit). For Suppliers, BILLS (Purchases) are negative (Credit). The code handles this "Inversion" logic to show the correct perspective for the user.

4. CORE BUSINESS LOGIC & FLOW
1. Generation: At month-end, the user clicks "Generate Bill". The system identifies "Unbilled Customers" (Active agreement + Stock in hand).
2. Automation: The system generates individual bill line items by replaying the month's challans.
3. Posting: Upon "Finalizing", the system creates a `Bill` record AND a matching `Transaction` record automatically.
4. Accountability: No bill can be "Deleted" without also deleting its ledger entry, ensuring the "Dues List" is never out of sync with actual invoices.
