MODULE: COMPANY & SETTINGS - EXHAUSTIVE DETAILS

1. OVERVIEW
The Company & Settings module is the administrative core of StockDo. It manages the identity of the firm (Logo, GSTIN, Address) and the legal framework (Agreement Templates) that governs all hire contracts. It also defines global financial behaviors, such as how taxes are applied to logistics.

2. DATABASE TABLES
--- Table: company ---
* Purpose: Stores the global identity and configuration of the business.
* Critical Fields:
  - companyName / logo: Used in headers of all generated Bills, Challans, and Quotations.
  - gstin: The firm's tax ID. If this is empty, the system automatically disables GST calculations globally.
  - gstOnTransportation (Boolean): A global toggle. If true, GST is calculated on (Rent + Transport). If false, it is only on (Rent).
  - fixedAssetsValue: Represents the initial "Capital" value of the warehouse/equipment for Balance Sheet calculations.
  - ledgerAccountId (UUID): The company's own unique identifier in the `transactions` table, used to track internal expenses and capital injections.

--- Table: agreement_templates ---
* Purpose: Centralizes the "Fine Print" of your contracts.
* Fields:
  - introText: The opening paragraph of the agreement (supports placeholders like {date}, {party1}, etc.).
  - terms (Json): An array of legal clauses.
  - isActive: Ensures only one template is used for new agreements at a time.

3. FILE-BY-FILE CODE EXPLANATION

--- backend/src/company/company.service.ts ---
* Logic: Singleton Pattern
  - Using `findFirst()` and `update/create` logic, the service ensures there is only ever one "Company" record.
  - Ledger Self-Identity: It automatically generates a `randomUUID()` for its own `ledgerAccountId` during the first setup. This allows the system to treat the "Company" as a party in transactions (e.g., when the owner injects capital).

--- backend/src/agreement-templates/agreement-templates.service.ts ---
* Logic: Database Seeding (`onModuleInit`)
  - The system is "Self-Healing". On the very first boot, it checks if any templates exist. If not, it hard-codes and inserts a "Standard Agreement" with 8 default legal clauses.
* Logic: Template placeholders
  - The templates use a placeholder system (`{siteAddress}`, `{minimumRentPeriod}`, etc.) which are dynamically replaced by the `AgreementsModule` when printing a PDF for a customer.

--- frontend/src/app/dashboard/company/page.tsx ---
* Logic: Global Config UI
  - Provides a single-point-of-entry to update the company's physical and financial identity.
  - Changes here immediately propagate to all billing and reporting modules.

4. CORE BUSINESS LOGIC & FLOW
1. Initial Setup: User enters company details. The system generates an internal ledger ID and seeds the first agreement template.
2. Configuration: User toggles "GST on Transportation" based on their auditor's advice.
3. Propagation: Every new Bill generated by the `BillingService` now references the `company.gstin` to decide if tax should be applied.
4. Compliance: Every new Agreement created for a customer automatically pulls the `introText` and `terms` from the current active template in this module.
