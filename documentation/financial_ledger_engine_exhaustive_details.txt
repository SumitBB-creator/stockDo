MODULE: GLOBAL FINANCIAL LEDGER & TRANSACTION ENGINE - EXHAUSTIVE DETAILS

1. OVERVIEW
This module is the "General Ledger" and financial heart of StockDo. It unifies every physical entity (Customer, Supplier, Employee, and the Company itself) into a single accounting framework. It ensures that every rupee moving in or out of the business is tracked with a permanent, auditable transaction record.

2. THE "LAID" (LEDGER ACCOUNT ID) SYSTEM
* Concept: To prevent ID overlap, the system uses a Global Ledger ID system (`LAIDXXXXXX`).
* Generation Logic: 
  - Found in `EmployeesService.create()` and similar methods.
  - The system performs a "Max ID Search" across all three tables (Customers, Employees, Suppliers).
  - It extracts the numeric part of the highest existing LAID and increments it.
* Purpose: This allows the `Transaction` table to use a single `ledgerAccountId` field that could point to any person or business in the ecosystem, simplifying global reports.

3. DYNAMIC BALANCE MIRRORING
The `TransactionService` implements a critical "Mirroring" logic to handle the dual nature of business relationships:
* Customer/Employee Ledger:
  - BILL / DEBIT_NOTE: Increases Balance (+ Debit).
  - RECEIPT / PAYMENT / CREDIT_NOTE: Decreases Balance (- Credit).
* Company/Supplier Ledger:
  - PURCHASE / DEBIT_NOTE: Decreases Balance (- Credit).
  - PAYMENT / RECEIPT / CREDIT_NOTE: Increases Balance (+ Debit).
* This logic ensures the "Balance" column in a Ledger Statement always reflects the "Amount Receivable" or "Amount Payable" correctly for that specific entity type.

4. CORE ANALYTICAL ENGINES

--- Ledger Statement Builder (`getLedger`) ---
* Logic: Resolves the "Party" by searching 4 tables (Customer, Supplier, Employee, Company).
* Logic: Calculates "Opening Balance" by summing every transaction date-stamped before the requested "From Date".
* Logic: Enriches rows by resolving UUIDs to human-readable "Bill Numbers" or "Voucher Numbers".

--- Dueslist Engine (`getDueslist`) ---
* Logic: Uses Postgres Group-By to aggregate net balances for all parties simultaneously.
* Logic: Implements "Advance Payment Detection". If Net Due < 0, it categorizes the amount as an "Advance," which is recorded as a Liability on the Balance Sheet.

--- Balance Sheet Engine (`getBalanceSheet`) ---
* Logic: Assets = Fixed Assets + Liquid Cash + (Closing Stock x Last Purchase Rate) + Debtors.
* Logic: Liabilities = Creditors + Advances from Customers.
* Real-time Valuation: It dynamically calculates "Stock Value" by looking up the most recent `PurchaseItem` rate for every material in inventory and multiplying it by current `totalQty`.

5. CORE BUSINESS LOGIC & FLOW
1. Event: A bill is finalized or a payment is received.
2. Hook: The calling service (Billing/Receipts) invokes `transactionsService.create()`.
3. Recording: A transaction is saved with a type-specific prefix (R-0001, P-0005) and a permanent point-in-time timestamp.
4. Visibility: The business owner opens the "Dueslist" to see a sorted list of highest-debt customers, or the "Daybook" to audit every financial action taken by staff today.
